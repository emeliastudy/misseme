Reflection Note

The lesson aimed to teach vocabulary through instructions. Students responded well, but I noticed some confusion with complex sentences. Next time, I'll use simpler words and check understanding more often.

Giving Instructions

Use clear, simple sentences like "Please open your books." Check understanding with "Do you understand?"

Questioning Strategies

Ask open-ended questions like "What do you think?" to encourage thinking. Use wait time for answers.

Managing Behaviour

Say "Eyes on me" for attention. Praise good behaviour with "Well done!" to keep students focused.

Giving Feedback

Give positive feedback like "Great job!" and constructive tips like "Try again with more details."
